Our

Mission

To create an adaptive platform that dynamically seeks to address the evolving challenges associated with capital sourcing for start-up fund seekers and marginalized transformationalists through the use of technology

Our

Vision

To be the leader e-capital sourcing platform in Africa serving access to capital businesses and fund seekers across all social-economic sectors

Our

Core Values

  • (i)Simplicity: to provide simplified solutions to fund seekers and investors
  • (ii)Adaptiveness: to proactively adapt to the changing economic environments in providing our solutions.
  • (iii)Social consciousness: to always be driven by the desire to better the lives of those that impacted by our activities
