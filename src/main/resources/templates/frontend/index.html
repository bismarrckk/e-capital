About Us

Seed Capital is an online enabler for start-ups to access quick capital through a simplified technology- driven platform. The Seed initiative was born in a realization of the challenges Startup businesses go through to access financing. Seed also borrows strongly from the realization that there are willing investors or donors willing to support these business but the link between these two parties barely exists. Web-based solutions present an agile platform for businesses to cut down red tape associated with traditional systems and allow business access resources otherwise unavailable due to its ubiquitous nature. Seed therefore affords such startups an opportunity to reach out to global investor community to fund and expand their local businesses. See more on our company profile
